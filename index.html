Frequently asked questions

Which dice can I roll?

All standard polyhedral dice: d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and d100. You can build mixed pools and roll several at once.

How do I roll multiple dice at once?

Tap the dice you want to add them to your pool, then tap the dice area or shake your device to roll. The free app supports pools of up to 5 dice; Pocket Polyhedral Pro raises the cap to 20.

What does Pocket Polyhedral Pro include?

A one-time $1.99 purchase (no subscriptions) unlocks pools of up to 20 dice, plus saved pools and custom dice sizes. The free app has no ads and includes mixed pools, advantage/disadvantage, modifiers, and history.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Settings inside the app and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How does advantage / disadvantage work?

Set the mode to Advantage or Disadvantage and roll. The app rolls two d20s and keeps the higher (advantage) or lower (disadvantage) result.

Are the rolls actually random?

Yes. Results use the system's cryptographic random number generator and are uniformly distributed across each die's faces.

Does the app collect my data?

No. There are no ads, no tracking, and no accounts. Your roll history is stored only on your device and never leaves it.

Privacy

Pocket Polyhedral does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data, including roll history, stays on your device. The only network activity is the standard App Store in-app purchase transaction handled by Apple.
