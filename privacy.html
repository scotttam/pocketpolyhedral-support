Privacy Policy

Pocket Polyhedral

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Pocket Polyhedral ("the app") is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

Information we collect

None. Pocket Polyhedral does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not require an account, and we do not track you.

Data stored on your device

The app stores your settings and your roll history locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed if you delete the app.

Analytics and tracking

The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking technologies of any kind.

In-app purchases

Pocket Polyhedral offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Pro"). Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Network access

The app works fully offline. The only network activity is the standard App Store transaction handled by Apple when you make or restore an in-app purchase.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
